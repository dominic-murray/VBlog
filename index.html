Quick Recipies

Quick Breakfast

Slice of bread
1 tomato
1 clove of garlic
pinch of salt

1. Toast the bread both sides.
2. While Still hot scrub the garlic along one side of the toast.
3. Grate the tomato or if you have blender pules the tomato mix in pinch of salt.
4. Cover the garlic side in tomato mixture and enjoy.lemon

Quick Tomato Soup

3-4 tomatos
tomato paste
lemon
parsley
celery
vegtable stock

1. put tomatos, tomato paste and stock into the blender blend till liquid.
2. Put mixture into a pot and heat till boiling then lower heat and let it simmer.
3. Put soup in bowl. Chop up parsley and celery to garnish and a squeeze of lemon, may also sprinkle with chili flakes.
